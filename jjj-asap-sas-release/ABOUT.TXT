
ABOUT.TXT
=========

This software was used to generate a winning entry in a contest. You can read more about
the contest here: http://www.kaggle.com/c/asap-sas.

This software is very specific to the contest format. It was also developed very quickly
and is more of a work in progress than a finished product. Albeit, the winners of the
contest were required by the rules to release their code. So here it is.
